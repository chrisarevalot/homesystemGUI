1. When in Leave Home Mode all the sensors of the house are secured. A timer of 30 seconds will be started, enough for you to leave the house. Otherwise the alarm will go off and the security pin will be asked. 
2. In Leave Home Mode, when opening the door another 30 seconds timer will be started, enough for you to input the passcode. Otherwise the alarm will go off. 
3. When in Stay Home Mode the perimeter sensors of the house are secured. No timer will be started. 
4. In Stay Home Mode if any sensor becomes unsecured (opened) the alarm will go off. A 30 second timer will start, enough for you to input the passcode. Otherwise a notification will be sent to the authorities.
